Welcome to The Silly Spot

A Cocktail Bar for Clowns, by Clowns

Step right up! The Silly Spot is the premier watering hole for jesters, jokers, and merry misfits alike. Whether you’re a seasoned circus act or just clowning around, our vintage-inspired cocktails are no laughing matter—crafted with flair, served with a wink, and always a little over the top.

Leave your big shoes at the door, grab a pastel-hued drink, and let's get silly!

The Clown Code

Serious Clowns Only
(but not too serious!)

If you’ve made it past the red nose checkpoint, congratulations—you belong here! At The Silly Spot, we live by a few simple rules: keep the honks friendly, the tricks lighthearted, and the drinks delightfully over-the-top.

No party poopers, only party starters. Whether you juggle, joke, or just enjoy a good giggle, all clowns are welcome—as long as you bring the fun. Now, grab a drink, mind your oversized shoes, and remember: the silliest clowns get the best service!
